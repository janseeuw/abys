Discover My Work

As a dedicated marine biologist and National Geographic Explorer, I focus on understanding and preserving marine ecosystems through innovative research and conservation efforts. My work spans from deep-sea exploration to coastal habitat restoration, with a particular emphasis on endangered marine species.

Learn More

About Me

With over a decade of experience in marine biology research, I've dedicated my career to understanding and protecting our ocean ecosystems. My fieldwork has taken me from the coral reefs of the Pacific to the deep-sea trenches of the Atlantic.

Through my role as a National Geographic Explorer, I combine scientific research with storytelling to raise awareness about marine conservation issues and inspire the next generation of ocean advocates.
